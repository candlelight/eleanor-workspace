## Main Sections

The planner is organized into **5 main life areas** at the top:

1. **Goals** 🎯
2. **Health & Wellness** 🌿
3. **Work & Projects** 💼
4. **Home Life** 🏠
5. **Memory & Reflection** 📸

Each section has customizable photos that can be replaced with Canva templates.

---

## Key Features & Databases

### 📅 Monthly Calendar
- Central hub for events and appointments
- Syncs with Weekly Calendar View
- Events appear as "Main Event" tags

### ✅ Monthly To-Do List
- Links to Yearly and Quarterly Goals
- Tasks with due dates auto-populate from goals
- Serves as monthly priority overview

### 🌸 Monthly Vision Board
- Canva template for creating monthly moodboards
- Upload custom images for visual inspiration

### 📋 Weekly Task Manager
- Week-by-week task tracking
- Completed tasks move to "Completed Tasks View"
- Designed for "dopamine hits" when checking things off

### 🌱 Weekly Habit Tracker
- Connected to Monthly Habit Tracker Page
- Daily habit entries with checkboxes
- Progress tracking over time

### 💪 Weekly Workout Log
- Linked to Fitness Hub Page
- Tracks:
  - Sets & reps
  - Duration
  - Energy level
  - Reflections/notes
- Monthly workout overview in Fitness Hub

### 📝 Content Planner Database
- For social media/content creation
- Tracks:
  - Post titles
  - Platforms (LinkedIn, TikTok, Newsletter, Pinterest, YouTube, IG)
  - Categories (Article, Pin, Video, Post, Story, Reel, Carousel)
  - Pillars (Soft Productivity, Cozy Desk, Freelancing, Digital Planning)
  - Status (Post ✅, Scheduled 📆, Draft ✏️, Idea 🌱)
  - Completion tracking

### 📚 Resources/Learning Database
- Tracks books, courses, professional development
- Fields: Resource Title, Type, Platform, Category, Status, Start Date, Notes
- Example entry: "Life Brief" (book, Indie platform, Professional Development category)

### 📺 Watch List Database
- TV shows and movies tracking
- Fields: Name, Type, Status, Platform, Genres, My Rating, Date Watched
- Example entry: "Amazing Hotels, Life Beyond the Lobby" (BBC series)
