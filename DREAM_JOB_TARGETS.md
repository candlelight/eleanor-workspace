## 🎯 IDEAL MANAGER SCREENING QUESTIONS

Use these in interviews to identify sponsors, not just supervisors:

1. **"Tell me about a time you helped someone on your team get visibility with senior leadership."** (Tests: Do they open doors?)

2. **"How do you typically handle organizational politics that might get in the way of your team's work?"** (Tests: Will they shield you?)

3. **"What's the hardest problem you're hoping this hire will solve?"** (Tests: Do they give hard problems + space?)

4. **"How do you prefer to give feedback? Can you give me an example?"** (Tests: Regular, honest coaching?)

5. **"Who on your team has grown the most since you started managing them, and what did that look like?"** (Tests: Do they develop people?)

**Red flags:**
- Can't name specific examples
- Talks about "managing up" instead of protecting team
- Wants someone to "execute my vision" (no space for your approach)
- Vague about feedback process
