## 📚 Trading Education

### Acronyms Decoded (Plain English)

**OHLCV** - "What happened today?"
- **O**pen: Price at 9:30 AM market open
- **H**igh: Highest price of the day
- **L**ow: Lowest price of the day
- **C**lose: Price at 4:00 PM close
- **V**olume: How many shares traded

**SMA** - Simple Moving Average ("The trend line")
- SMA 20 = Average price over last 20 days
- SMA 50 = Average price over last 50 days
- Price above SMA = uptrend, below = downtrend

**RSI** - Relative Strength Index ("Overbought or oversold?")
- Scale of 0-100
- **>70** = Overbought (might drop)
- **<30** = Oversold (might bounce)
- **~50** = Neutral

**MACD** - Moving Average Convergence Divergence ("Momentum shift")
- Crosses above zero = bullish momentum
- Crosses below zero = bearish momentum

## 🚦 Signal Guide

### Buy Signals (🟢)
| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Golden Cross** | SMA 20 crosses ABOVE SMA 50 |
| **RSI < 30** | Oversold (beaten down, potential bounce) |
| **MACD crosses up** | Momentum turning positive |

### Sell Signals (🔴)
| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Death Cross** | SMA 20 crosses BELOW SMA 50 |
| **RSI > 70** | Overbought (too hot, might drop) |
| **MACD crosses down** | Momentum turning negative |

---

## 🎯 6-Step Decision Framework

1. **Check Trend** (SMA) - Price above or below?
2. **Check Momentum** (RSI) - Overbought/oversold?
3. **Check MACD** - Confirming momentum?
4. **Check Volume** - High conviction?
5. **Check Candlesticks** - Reversal patterns?
6. **DECIDE:** 3+ bullish = BUY | 3+ bearish = SELL | Mixed = WAIT

---

## ⚙️ Trading Rules

### Entry Criteria (Portfolio B)
- Golden Cross + RSI < 50 + MACD crossing up
- All three aligning = high confidence

### Exit Criteria
- Death Cross (trend reversal)
- RSI > 70 (overbought)
- -10% stop loss (automatic)

### Profit Taking
- **25% gain** = conservative take profit
- **50% gain** = if parabolic/unusual trend

### Risk Management
- Max 10% loss per trade
- Split $100 across 2-3 positions
- Diversify sectors
